Who We Are

THE WAKE-UP CALL INT'L is a purpose-driven, faith-inspired development initiative committed to awakening individuals to personal responsibility, proactive living, and excellence. Inspired by the message of Matthew 13:25 — "While men slept..." — our work addresses a critical reality facing individuals and societies today: too many lives and systems decline not for lack of potential, but because of delayed awareness and inaction.

What We Do

Academic & Career Guidance

Providing mentorship and strategic planning for educational and professional pathways.

Personal Growth & Development

Equipping individuals with tools for self-awareness, discipline, and character development.

Leadership Development

Cultivating visionary, ethical leaders committed to service and transformation.

Performance Improvement

Enhancing productivity and effectiveness through accountability systems and goal setting.
