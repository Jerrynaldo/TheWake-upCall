Founder & President

Hassan Mansaray

Hassan Mansaray is the Founder and President of THE WAKE-UP CALL INT'L, a faith-inspired development initiative focused on youth empowerment, leadership development, and performance transformation.

With over 15 years of experience in community development and youth mentorship, Hassan has dedicated his life to awakening potential in individuals and communities. His approach combines practical life skills with spiritual principles to create sustainable transformation.

Hassan holds a Master's degree in Organizational Leadership and is a certified life coach. He is passionate about equipping the next generation with the tools they need to thrive in an increasingly complex world.
