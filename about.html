About Us

Who We Are

THE WAKE-UP CALL INT'L is a purpose-driven, faith-inspired development initiative committed to awakening individuals to personal responsibility, proactive living, and excellence. Inspired by the message of Matthew 13:25 — "While men slept..." — our work addresses a critical reality facing individuals and societies today: too many lives and systems decline not for lack of potential, but because of delayed awareness and inaction.

Founded with a strong conviction for transformation, The Wake-Up Call INT'L responds to the growing culture of passivity, procrastination, blame-shifting, and inaction that limits progress, particularly among young people.

Our Story

The idea behind The Wake-Up Call INT'L emerged from firsthand engagement with young people, professionals, and aspiring leaders across communities. A recurring pattern became evident — individuals struggling not primarily because of lack of opportunity, but due to the absence of discipline, urgency, accountability, and self-awareness.

This realization sparked a movement to address the root causes of stagnation and underachievement. We believe that every individual possesses untapped potential waiting to be awakened through intentional development and faith-inspired principles.

Our approach combines practical life skills with character development and spiritual grounding, creating a holistic framework for sustainable transformation.
